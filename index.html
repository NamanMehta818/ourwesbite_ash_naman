Who we are

OpenSite Labs is a nonprofit collective of designers and developers dedicated to closing the digital divide. We take communities and small businesses online with modern, accessible, and performance-first websites.

Founded on the belief that every mission deserves a strong web presence, we donate our time, tools, and talent. You bring the story. We build the site.
